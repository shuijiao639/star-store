About Dragon Sweeper

Dragon Sweeper is an exciting puzzle game that combines strategy and quick thinking. Your mission is to navigate through fields while avoiding hidden dragons. Use clues to identify safe spaces and mark potential dangers!

This game takes inspiration from classic puzzle games but adds a fantasy twist with dragons instead of mines. Perfect for players of all ages who enjoy challenging their minds.

How to Play

  • Left-click to reveal a tile
  • Right-click to mark a suspected dragon
  • Numbers indicate how many dragons are in the adjacent tiles
  • Clear all non-dragon tiles to win the game
  • Be careful! If you reveal a dragon, the game is over

Game Features

Multiple Difficulty Levels

Challenge yourself with different board sizes and dragon densities. From beginner to expert, there's a perfect level for everyone!

Engaging Gameplay

Each game provides a unique challenge that will test your logical thinking and deduction skills in exciting ways.

Fantasy Theme

Enjoy a fantasy-inspired design with dragons and medieval elements that make the classic puzzle format more immersive.

Play Anywhere

Dragon Sweeper works on desktop and mobile devices, so you can enjoy the game whenever and wherever you want!

Tips & Tricks

  • Start with the corners and edges where there are fewer adjacent tiles
  • When you see a "1", there's only one dragon in the eight surrounding tiles
  • Use the process of elimination to identify safe tiles
  • Don't guess unless you absolutely have to!
  • Look for patterns in the numbers to make logical deductions
